Haoyu Liu

Ph.D. Student

College of Control Science and Engineering, Zhejiang University

Email: haoyu_liu@zju.edu.cn


I started my Ph.D. at ZJU in 2017, under the supervision of Prof. Jiming Chen and Prof. Shibo he. Currently I am working as a visiting Ph.D. student at SUNY, Buffalo, under the advisory of Prof. Jing Gao. Before joining ZJU, I got my Master of Science degree in Data Science from King's College London and got my Bachelor of Engineering degree in Control Science and Engineering from Zhejiang University.


I am broadly interested in data mining and machine learning, with particular interests in Anomaly Detection and its applications in large-scale systems.
